What is Sound Healing?

Sound Healing involves using sounds and their harmonic vibrations to improve a person's health and well-being. Sound therapies are not just a New Age trend, they have been prevalent in cultures around the world from the local Native Americans to ancient Greece, Tibet and Egypt.

There are many benefits to Sound Healing as it can assist in improving the quality of life in people living with anxiety, depression, stress, pain, as well as aiding recovery in many other ailments. It can also lead to improved memory, creativity and relaxation. For example, Buddhist monks have used Tibetan Singing Bowls to aid their meditation for centuries.

Today, Wisdom Keepers with training and experience in Sound Healing offer many different types of therapy, including but not limited to the use of Tibetan Singing Bowls, Gongs, Tuning Forks and Guided Meditation to bring inner harmony.
